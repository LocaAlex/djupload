About Djupload

Djupload is a secure and user-friendly file upload platform built using the Django web framework. Our goal is to provide a seamless experience for users to upload, manage, and download their files with ease. Hereâ€™s what you can do with Djupload:

  • Upload Files: Easily upload files of various types through a simple and intuitive interface. Our platform ensures that your files are stored securely and are only accessible to you.
  • Manage Your Files: View a list of all the files you have uploaded. You can manage your files directly from your dashboard, with options to delete files you no longer need.
